FOODS AND CUISINE IN KARNATAKA

Bisi Bele Bath

Ragi Mudde

Finger millet balls that are often served with sambar or curry. Ragi is a staple grain in rural Karnataka and is known for its health benefits.

Mysore Pak

A delicious sweet made from ghee, sugar, and gram flour. It has a rich, melt-in-the-mouth texture and is a popular dessert.

Dosa

A fermented crepe made from rice and urad dal batter. Variants include Masala Dosa (stuffed with spiced potatoes) and Set Dosa (thicker and softer).
