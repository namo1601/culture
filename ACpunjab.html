THE ARTS AND CRAFTS IN PUNJAB

Phulkari

A traditional embroidery technique characterized by colorful floral patterns on shawls and dupattas. Each piece tells a story and carries cultural significance.

Khes

Handwoven blankets made from wool or cotton, typically used in colder months.

Terracotta

Traditional clay pots and figurines that reflect local craftsmanship and everyday life.

Carving

Carving in Punjab is a traditional craft that showcases skilled artisanship and intricate designs.
