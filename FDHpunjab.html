FOODS AND CUISINE IN PUNJAB

Buutter Chiicken

A sweet flatbread stuffed with a mixture of jaggery and split gram (chana dal), typically served during festivals.

Sarson da Saag and Makki di Roti

Sarson da Saag and Makki di Roti are quintessential dishes from Punjab, celebrated for their rich flavors and wholesome ingredients

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a beloved North Indian dish, especially popular in Punjab. It features spicy chickpeas (chole) served with deep-fried bread (bhature).

Lassi

A traditional yogurt-based drink, which can be sweet or salty. Sweet lassi is often flavored with fruit or sugar.
