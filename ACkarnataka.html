THE ART AND CRAFTS IN KARNATAKA

Ilkal Sarees

Known for their unique borders and vibrant colors, these handwoven sarees are made using traditional techniques in the Ilkal region.

Channapatna Toys

These colorful, hand-turned wooden toys are made using traditional techniques and are recognized for their vibrant colors and designs.

Bidri ware

Bidriware is a metal handicraft from the city of Bidar in Karnataka, India.

Natyashastra

The Natyashastra has significantly influenced the performing arts in Karnataka, particularly in dance and theater.
