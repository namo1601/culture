THE LANGUAGE OF PUNJAB

Punjabi

Marathi is the official language of Maharashtra and one of the 22 scheduled languages of India. Here are some key points about Marathi:

Family:
Punjabi belongs to the Indo-Aryan group of the Indo-European language family.

Scripts:
In India, Punjabi is primarily written in the Gurmukhi script, while in Pakistan, it is often written in the Shahmukhi script, which is a variant of the Perso-Arabic script.

Cultural Significance:
Punjabi has a rich literary tradition, including poetry, folk songs, and prose. Notable poets such as Bulleh Shah and Waris Shah have contributed significantly to Punjabi literature.

Saraiki
